FlappyJet Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to FlappyJet! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game application ("FlappyJet" or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Nickname: You can choose a custom nickname for your profile (optional)
  • Game Progress: Your scores, achievements, and game statistics

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Game Data: High scores, game sessions, jet selections, and in-game purchases
  • Technical Data: App version, crash reports, and performance metrics
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the game features and menus

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Real name or personal identification
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts or social media accounts

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the game experience
  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Display leaderboards and achievements
  • Analyze game performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent cheating and ensure fair play
  • Provide customer support when needed

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most game data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Backup: Some progress data may be backed up to secure cloud servers
  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: We implement strict access controls to protect your data

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate the game (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

5.1 Third-Party Services

FlappyJet may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For cloud storage and analytics
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

FlappyJet is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We take special care to protect children's privacy:

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No chat features or social interactions with strangers
  • No behavioral advertising targeted at children
  • Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's data
  • All data collection is limited to what's necessary for game functionality

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Delete: Request deletion of your game data
  • Correct: Update or correct your nickname and preferences
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics or data collection (may affect game features)
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your game data

8. Data Retention

  • Game progress data is retained as long as you have the app installed
  • Leaderboard data may be retained for up to 2 years for fair competition
  • Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized after 12 months
  • You can delete your data at any time through the app settings

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data according to this privacy policy and applicable laws.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice through the app or app store

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@flappyjet.com
  • App Name: FlappyJet
  • Developer: Erez Kohanski
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

12. Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Policy
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws
